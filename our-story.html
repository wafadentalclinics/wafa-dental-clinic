Our Story

The foundation of trust, built on smiles.

From Fear to Fascination

Dr. Muhammad Waleed, Owner
Lead Dental Surgeon

As a child, I remember the dentist’s chair as a place of fear: the unfamiliar sounds, the clinical smells, the faces I didn’t know. Yet, what began as fear soon turned into fascination. That curiosity drew me into the world of dentistry, and before I knew it, I was immersed in dental college with long nights of study, clinical practice, and the realization that this profession is about far more than teeth.

Dentistry, I discovered, is about people. It’s about trust, comfort, and the confidence that comes from a healthy smile. That lesson became the foundation of WAFA Dental Clinic. From day one, my vision has been to create a space where patients feel welcomed, respected, and cared for on a personal level, a clinic that feels less like a medical appointment and more like being among friends who truly understand your needs.

Today, our team continues to grow, but our philosophy has never changed. We blend advanced dental techniques with compassion and a gentle touch, ensuring that every patient feels at ease from the moment they walk in.

Every smile we treat becomes part of our journey. And it is our greatest honor to be part of yours, helping you smile brighter, healthier, and with complete confidence.

From Vision to Reality

Dr. Fatima Asif Khan, Co-Owner
General Dentist

When I first met Waleed, I recognized more than a skilled young dentist. I saw someone with a vision, not simply to establish a clinic, but to create a place defined by compassion, trust, and care for every patient who walked through its doors.

At the time, I was deeply committed to my own path in dentistry, driven by a passion to help people feel confident in their smiles. As our journeys aligned, so too did our aspirations, and eventually, our lives. We married, and soon after, I joined Waleed not only as a partner in life but also as a partner in purpose. Together, we set out to build something greater than ourselves: a clinic that embodied our shared values, our passion for dentistry, and our names.

That vision became WAFA Dental Clinic. "WA" represents Waleed, and "FA" represents Fatima. Every detail of WAFA reflects our story. From the way patients are welcomed to the way treatments are designed, our clinic was built to feel less like a conventional practice and more like an extension of family, because that is how it began.

Working side by side, combining our skills and strengths, is a privilege we never take for granted. Together, we have created an environment where patients feel seen, heard, and genuinely cared for, where fear transforms into trust and appointments evolve into lasting relationships.

WAFA Dental Clinic is more than a name. It is our legacy, our passion, and a living testament to what is possible when two people share one vision.

A Legacy of Care and Commitment

Zafar Iqbal
Father of Muhammad Waleed

When my son was just a little boy, he was terrified of going to the dentist. I still remember how he would cling to my hand in the waiting room, his eyes wide with nervousness. Yet in those moments, I did not focus on his fear. Instead, I dreamed of a future. I imagined a day when he would wear the white coat, not as a patient, but as a healer. As my only son, I quietly hoped he might one day choose dentistry, not only for the profession, but for the life and legacy we could build together.

I never pushed him. But when he eventually chose dentistry on his own, my heart filled with pride. I saw in him not just a student, but someone who truly cared about people. He wanted to ease the very fears he had once felt himself.

Watching him grow through university, face challenges, study late into the night, and emerge stronger each time was the greatest reward a father could ask for. And when we finally opened the doors to the clinic, it was not just the start of a business. It was the fulfillment of a father’s dream and the result of a son’s hard-earned achievement.

Today, WAFA Dental Clinic stands as more than a dental practice. It is a place built on love, legacy, and the belief that every patient deserves to be treated like family. Every smile Waleed creates, every patient he helps feel safe and confident, is a reminder of how far we have come.

This is our story: a father’s dream, a son’s journey, and a shared commitment to caring for others, one smile at a time.
